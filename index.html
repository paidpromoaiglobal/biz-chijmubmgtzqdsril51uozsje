About Shortcut unisex salon

Shortcut Unisex Salon, nestled in the heart of Saibaba Colony, Coimbatore, has earned its reputation as one of the city's finest grooming destinations. With a stellar 4.9-star rating backed by over 1,249 happy clients, we bring together skilled stylists, a welcoming atmosphere, and a passion for making every guest look and feel their absolute best. Whether you're walking in for a quick trim or a complete style makeover, our team is dedicated to delivering perfection.

Our talented stylists — including the much-loved Sakthi, Giri, and Kutty Santhosh — are known for their expertise, friendly approach, and genuine dedication to their craft. We take pride in understanding each client's unique hair type and personal style to recommend and create looks that truly suit them. Open seven days a week from 7 AM to 10 PM, Shortcut Unisex Salon is always here when you need us — because great hair should never have to wait.
